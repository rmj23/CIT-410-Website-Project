Welcome to SugarScience!

In order to get accurate and personalized results we need a little bit of information to begin. Below you will find a form requesting some personal bio metric information such as your height and weight. This information is not collected and used only to calculate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) this number just represents the total number of calories you need a day in order to maintain your current body composition.

We use your TDEE to generate some guidelines about how much sugar you should be eating based on the total number of calories you need a day. If your sugar intake is taking up a significant portion of your daily calories the risk for certain diseases can increase dramatically.

Your activity level would be depend on the amount of exercise experienced per week. A sedentary rating would mean no exercise while an extremely active would represent exercising everyday along with large amounts of activity. An activity rating of moderate would be exercising about four times a week.
